Welcome to loseit.io

Founded in 2018 in the MIE common room, loseit.io is an application designed to help users reach their weight loss goals. Everybody is different, and our team works to embrace differences by delivering personally tailored workout plans that cater to your excercise preferences. Our algorithms carefully account for each individuals likes and dislikes, ideal workout durations, intensity, and dieting habits.
